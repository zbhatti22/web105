    How to Make a Pizza

      type= "1"
    1. Ingredients
      1. Pizza dough
      2. Pizza sauce
      3. Cheese (mozzarella is recommended)
      4. Toppings of your choice
        • Pepperoni
        • Mushrooms
        • Green peppers
        • Onions
        • Olives
    2. Herbs and spices
      1. Oregano
      2. Basil
      3. Crushed red pepper flakes
  • Instructions
    1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
    2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
    3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
    4. Sprinkle a generous amount of cheese over the sauce.
    5. Add your chosen toppings.
    6. Season with oregano, basil, and crushed red pepper flakes to taste.
    7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
    8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
    9. Slice and serve your delicious homemade pizza!
