RENAISSANCE

By Beyonce

Cover Image from Imgur.com

RENAISSANCE by Beyonce is a musical adventure. It takes you on a roller coaster of emotions. There is a mixture of genres on the album but the most common is house music which is sampled throughout the project. She has been touring the album for a couple months now and it will come to an end shortly.

Themes

the album has a theme of self love and feeling confident in who you are. Songs like "I'M THAT GIRL, ALIEN SUPERSTAR & COZY" do a good job of this. Their upbeat beats and empowering lyrics make for a powerful duo
